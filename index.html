Guildenstern

Details

  • Occupation: Fixer, Socialite
  • Race/Gender: Human Male
  • Age: 187
  • Disposition: Jovial, loves drink, loves debauchery of all kinds really. He is fabulously wealthy from a life of adventuring, and he spends his retirement as a socialite and fixer. He is happy to trade war stories with adventurers
  • Appearance: A man of prodigious girth garbed in crushed green velvet pants and coat with gold tassels and buttons. A velvet cape of luminous gold is tied with golden rope around his neck. His chubby, ruddy face is covered by blonde mutton chops and ringlets of blonde hair drape over his shoulders under a jaunty black hat tipped to the side. A sheathed rapier dangles from a baldric on the side of his wide hips.

Backstory

Once known as the Old Wolf, Guildenstern made a fortune and carved out a reputation as an adventurer and philanderer. Today, an older and wiser Guildenstern serves as a "fixer" - a man who connects adventurers with clients in need of mercenaries. The years have not worn him down, and though he has grown soft in the flesh, he remains deceptively strong, vigorous, and clear of mind. Guildenstern has survived the passing of centuries by means of magic. Despite his prodigious girth, Guildenstern can move with good speed when he must, and he has not let his adventuring skills wither. His wife, Asper, passed away several years ago, and his rambling mansion has seen better days. Guildenstern spends his days embroiled in politics and whiles away his nights in drink and debauchery.
